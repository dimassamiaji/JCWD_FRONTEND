QUEEN

We love music

Queen is a British rock band formed in 1970 in London . Originally, they consisted of Freddie Mercury (vocals, piano), Brian May (guitar, vocals), Roger Meddows-Taylor (drums, vocals), and John Deacon (bass guitar). Queen's early works were influenced by progressive rock , hard rock and heavy metal , but they took gradual risks towards more conventional and radio listener-friendly works, such as arena rock and pop rock .
